About Me

My life has taken some interesting turns, eventually resulting in me enrolling in a full-stack, web-development course at the age of 41.

I currently live in Salt Lake City, Utah with my brilliant husband and a derpy, but gorgous, rescue mutt. My husband and I work together building, updating, and marketing our own websites. We are also avid travelers and spend at least one month every year in a foreign country.
